We are a charity volunteer organisation that was created once the problem of transit war refugees in Estonia become obvious.

A lot of families are disoriented, not knowing what to do and where to go. Some of them don't plan to stay here, they want to go to their relatives in Europe, or to another countries around the world, to start the new life.

We help as much as we can.

If you go to Estonia (transit or no), and you need help – please, fill in our questionnaire in advance, and we will try to contact you as fast as possible.

If you go to Estonia or transiting through, here are some examples of how can we help you:
  • Consult about available options for refugees in Estonia
  • Plan the optimal route and transport, suggest available options in another countries
  • Answer questions about transportation rules
  • Help with small issues on the spot, find something
  • Help with accommodation or tickets
  • Tell where and when to find the volunteers
  • Discuss non-standard questions and find a solution

    Transit refugees need help. Governmental support programs are designated only for those, who decide to stay in Estonia, thus many families come to us. The people flow do not decrease, families need attention and volunteer's help, both online and offline. We are ready to teach, help, answer questions and give all of our organisation's instruments to volunteers, so they can help the families.
