Helihool

Helihool MTÜ is an Estonian non-profit association established in 2019 in Tallinn. As per its Articles of Association, Heliihool MTÜ has the objective to make people lives better by the following means:

a. Helping war refugees:

• organizing transfers,
• tickets & food procurement,
• accommodation in Estonia,
• consultations,
• any other ways that may be needed to help the refugees.
