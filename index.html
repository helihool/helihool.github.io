Helihool Mittetulundusühing is an Estonian non-profit association established in 2019 in Tallinn. As per its Articles of Association, Helihool MTÜ has the objective to make people lives better by the following means:
a. Helping war refugees:

• organising transfers,
• tickets & food procurement,
• accommodation in Estonia,
• consultations,
• any other ways that may be needed to help the refugees

Learn more about "Mariupoli Sõbrad"
b. Making high-quality live music accessible

• for patients in hospitals and care homes,
• for war refugees,
• for kids,
• for the general public, who would get positive effect from the live music
