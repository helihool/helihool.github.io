We make high-quality life music accessible for patients in hospitals and care homes.

Helihool is a Tallinn-based initiative, created by Olga Bulavina in 2018.

For a year, our musicians delivered musical programs to the hospitals with support of the Estonian Academy of Music and Theatre.

We aim to expand and bring music to more health-care facilities across Tallinn and Estonia.
