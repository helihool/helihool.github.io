Who We Are

At Helihool Music, we believe in the power of music to uplift, heal, and inspire. From enchanting concerts in gardens to heartwarming performances in hospitals, we bring the magic of music to everyone.

Our vocal lessons are not just about hitting the right notes; they're about finding your voice and expressing your soul. Whether online or in-person, our individual and choir sessions are a celebration of harmony and self-expression.

Join us in spreading the joy of music to the world, one note at a time.

FAQ

How Can I Join?

Simply click on the 'Join Now' button and let the magic of music begin!

Are Kids Welcome?

Absolutely! We believe in nurturing a love for music from a young age, so bring the little ones along for a magical experience.

What's the Age Limit?

There's no age limit for experiencing the joy of music. Our events are designed to enchant and inspire music lovers of all ages.

Can I Request a Song?

We love surprises! While we can't guarantee every request, we'll do our best to sprinkle a little musical magic into your day.

Is Parking Available?

Yes, we've got you covered! Ample parking is available at all our event venues for your convenience.
