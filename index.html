Helihool is an initiative organised by former and current students of Estonian Academy of Music and Theatre.

Our main goal is to bring talented musicians from around the world to those who cannot attend live music concerts- mostly to hospice patients. 

We encourage young musicians to create interesting concert programs, that can be used outside of their academic institutions, and become a solid base for their professional career development.
We strive to bring community music to a level that can be appreciated and supported by the governmental organisations and various communities here in Estonia.
